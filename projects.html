Cork Airport Social Media Campaign

The Airport aimed to market share of 4% year on year and 2 share points by the end of the year through a social and digital campaign. My group in college created a FaceBook and Instagram campaigns called Everywhere from Home. The campaign would be promoted by micro-influencers who would live stream their journeys to different destination departuring from Cork Airport. Also, there would be online competition to encourage travel to the selected destinations. We got a great mark for the project.
